The Blind Spots

Your firewall is only half the picture

Firewalls block known bad traffic. But what about the threats they don't recognise? And what about your own data walking out the door?

↓ Inbound

Zero-Day Threats

New malware and phishing payloads your firewall hasn't seen before. We spot suspicious patterns before signatures exist.

↓ Inbound

Compromised Sites

Legitimate websites get hacked. Staff visit them daily. We flag traffic to newly-suspicious destinations.

↑ Outbound

Data Exfiltration

Malware doesn't steal gigabytes at once. It trickles data out slowly, hiding in normal traffic. We catch the pattern.

↑ Outbound

Shadow IT

Staff using Dropbox, WeTransfer, personal cloud storage. Your sensitive data leaving via services you don't control.

↔ Lateral

C2 Beaconing

Compromised machines check in with attackers regularly. Small packets, easy to miss. Our AI spots the rhythm.

Compliance
